Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about services, process and collaboration.

What services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of web development services including full-stack web development, mobile app development with Flutter, API and backend development, UI/UX design, and interactive web experiences. I specialize in modern technologies like Next.js, Python, React, Flutter, and GSAP.

What is your development process?

My development process follows these key steps: 1) Discovery and planning phase to understand your requirements, 2) Design and prototyping to visualize the solution, 3) Development and testing with regular updates, 4) Deployment and launch, and 5) Ongoing support and maintenance. I believe in transparent communication and agile methodologies.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application can take 2-6 months. Mobile apps usually require 1-3 months. I provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the development process.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance services after project completion. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and feature additions. I provide different support packages to suit your needs and budget.

What technologies do you work with?

I work with a wide range of modern technologies including Next.js, React, Vue.js, Python (Django, Flask, FastAPI), Node.js, Flutter, Dart, JavaScript, TypeScript, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Tailwind CSS, GSAP, and various cloud platforms. I stay updated with the latest technologies and best practices.

How do you handle project communication?

I maintain regular communication through your preferred channels (email, Slack, WhatsApp, or project management tools). I provide weekly progress updates, schedule regular check-in calls, and am always available to answer questions or discuss changes. I believe clear communication is key to project success.

What are your rates and payment terms?

My rates depend on project complexity, timeline, and specific requirements. I offer both hourly and project-based pricing. Payment terms typically include a 50% upfront payment and 50% upon completion, with milestone payments for larger projects. I'm happy to discuss pricing based on your specific needs.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, I work with clients from around the world. I'm based in Lagos, Nigeria, but I'm comfortable working across different time zones. I use various collaboration tools and communication platforms to ensure smooth remote collaboration regardless of location.
